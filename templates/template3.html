Something a lot of property owners don't know going in:

Building recertification in South Florida requires four separate inspections: structural, electrical, thermographic infrared (IR), and nighttime parking lot illumination. Most firms only handle one or two, which means hiring multiple consultants, coordinating separate timelines, and submitting reports that all have to align before the city approves anything.

When those reports come from different firms or they don't align, the city flags it, and the owner deals with the fallout.

We Handle All 4 Inspections:

  • Structural Inspection
  • Electrical Inspection
  • Thermographic (IR) Inspection
  • Parking Lot Illumination

We handle all four under one roof (one team, one submission, one point of contact from the first inspection through final city sign-off).
